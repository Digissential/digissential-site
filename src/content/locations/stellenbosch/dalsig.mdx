## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Laptop won’t charge?** Test a different outlet and inspect the charger tip for heat or wobble.  
- **Wi-Fi weak upstairs?** Move the router off the floor; mesh nodes near stairwells help a lot.  
- **Random pop-ups?** Don’t call numbers shown. Disconnect Wi-Fi and we’ll clean it safely.
