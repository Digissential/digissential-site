## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Black screen, fans spinning?** Test an external monitor—likely a display cable/panel issue.  
- **Running hot?** Power down, keep vents clear, and avoid soft surfaces.  
- **Pop-up claiming “virus detected”?** Don’t call the number. Disconnect Wi-Fi and we’ll clean it safely.
