## About De Zalze

**De Zalze** blends winelands scenery with secure, estate living—perfect for remote work and study. We help residents keep home offices reliable with transparent pricing, clear timelines, and practical guidance that prevents repeat issues.

### What we help with most in De Zalze

- **Whole-home Wi-Fi stability:** mesh tuning, channel selection, router placement  
- **Slow or failing laptops:** SSD upgrades, battery swaps, thermal servicing  
- **Security & hygiene:** malware cleanup, safe backups, phishing recovery  
- **Data recovery:** from failed drives or accidental deletions (POPIA-safe)  
- **Printer & scanner setup:** drivers, sharing, and permissions

## Landmarks & micro-areas we serve

- Main estate entrances and visitor parking areas  
- Residences across the golf estate precincts  
- Nearby hospitality and lodge accommodations within the estate

## Service highlights

- **Same-day diagnostics** with clear, written quotes  
- **POPIA-aligned handling** and chain-of-custody on request  
- **Pick-up & return** from the gate or agreed meeting point  
- **Genuine parts where available**; fit-for-purpose quality otherwise

## Quick tips if your home office just dropped offline

- **Power-cycle in order:** ONT/modem → router → mesh nodes (wait 2–3 minutes between)  
- **Check for overlap:** move nodes off metal cabinets and away from microwaves  
- **Note symptoms:** which rooms fail and at what times—this speeds optimisation
