## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Wet or muddy laptop from a hike?** Power off, unplug, and don’t charge—moisture plus power damages boards.  
- **Fan noise & heat near the hinge?** Likely dust or old thermal paste—avoid heavy tasks until serviced.  
- **Wi-Fi drops in back rooms?** Try a temporary mesh node or Ethernet-backhaul; we’ll design a stable layout.
