## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Black screen but fans spin?** Test with an external display—if it works, the display chain needs service.  
- **Wi-Fi drops upstairs?** Temporarily move a mesh node to the stair landing; we’ll map a permanent layout.  
- **Strange pop-ups or “support” hotlines?** Power off and don’t call—bring it in for safe malware removal.
