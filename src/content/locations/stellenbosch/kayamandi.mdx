## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Black screen but fans spin?** Try an external monitor—if it works, it’s likely a display cable/panel issue.  
- **Feels hot?** Power down and keep vents clear; avoid charging under bedding.  
- **Scam pop-up?** Don’t call the number—disconnect Wi-Fi and we’ll clean it safely.
