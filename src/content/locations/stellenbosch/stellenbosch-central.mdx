## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Screen gone black but fans spin?** Connect an external monitor—if it works, it’s likely a display cable/panel issue.  
- **Hot chassis & slowdowns?** Power down and avoid charging in a closed backpack; overheating can damage the battery.  
- **Suspicious pop-ups?** Don’t call numbers on the screen. Disconnect Wi-Fi and we’ll clean it properly.
