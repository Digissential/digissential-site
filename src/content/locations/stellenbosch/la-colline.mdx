## Quick tips if your device just failed

- **Black screen, fans on?** Try an external monitor—if it works, likely a display cable/panel issue.  
- **Feels hot?** Power down and avoid charging under bedding or in a pouch—heat speeds battery wear.  
- **Scam pop-up?** Don’t call the number shown. Disconnect Wi-Fi and we’ll clean it safely.
